Why Are We Here?

Film production is a dynamic and complex process that often involves numerous stages before a movie reaches its final form for audiences. However, not all projects make it to the silver screen. Some encounter hurdles along the way, leading to cancellations, remain unreleased, or are left unfinished, intriguing audiences with their potential unrealized stories. In exploring these cancelled, unreleased, and unfinished film projects, we uncover not only the challenges within the film industry but also the creative risks, collaborative efforts, and the delicate balance between artistry and business that shape the world of cinema.
